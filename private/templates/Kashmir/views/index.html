In addition to servicing reports and requests from the public, this platform provides support for operations management, coordination, and sharing of information to all NGO, individual, and government initiatives involved in relief and reconstruction efforts following the Kashmir floods of September 2014. It is an open platform available to all who are working on the ground. While a number of civil society formations are cooperating on this effort, software engineers and volunteers associated with ReviveKashmir have been responsible for building and maintaining the platform.

Click here for a list of organizations that are currently on Athewaas.
Use Athewaas (Kashmir Flood Coordination System) if you need help

When you submit a report or a request for assistance, we will match it to the nearest place(s) that can provide help and track the progress on it.
