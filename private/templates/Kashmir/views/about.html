About us

Athewaas (Kashmir Flood Coordination System) is a web based collaboration tool that addresses the common coordination problems from managing aid to managing volunteers. It helps relief organizations manage their data and view public data shared by other organizations, allows workers in the field as well as common people to send in requests, and helps map requests to goods and services provided by the relief organizations. Organizations can also request support from each other. It is built using Sahana Eden.

Revive Kashmir is the organisation behind the establishment and support of the Athewaas platform. Revive Kashmir is an organisation whose primary objective is empowerment of and coordination between entities for sustainable long term social and economic development. The main areas of focus are enabling information access, cross sector engagement, aid and project implementation coordination and technical expertise sourcing.
