Sahana

{{=T("Puget Sound Maritime Common Operating Picture (MCOP)")}}

Sahana is a component of the Puget Sound Maritime Common Operating Picture (MCOP) platform. Sahana can be used for both large scale disaster management and also also be used for day-to-day activities and operations.

Sahana is linked to Wrike so you can get your projects and tasks located on a map to share with others. You can use Sahana for other mapping applications as well. Sahana has many features and functions that you are welcome to use.
