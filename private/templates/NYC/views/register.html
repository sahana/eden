{{=T("Criteria for Admittance")}}

To access NYC:Prepared tools, your group or organization must meet the following criteria:

  • Present. Have a physical and active presence in the New York City area.
  • Relevant. Currently engaged in disaster relief, disaster recovery or community resilience work.
  • Reputable. Actively coordinating through an established network:
    • Staten Island Long Term Recovery Organization
    • Lower East Side Long Term Recovery Group
    • Brooklyn Long Term Recovery Group
    • New York City VOAD
    • Occupy Sandy Project Spokes Council

    Learn more about NYC:Prepared on our website and feel free to contact us
